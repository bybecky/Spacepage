If I Lived In Space

Would you like to see what it's like to live on different planets? Fly with me and see.

On Mercury,

you can celebrate your birthday every 3 months.

  • Smallest planet
  • Has wrinkles that are miles
    high and long
  • Has rocky cliffs, valleys
    and crators
  • 1 day = 60 earth days
  • At night it is 1000 ℉

On Venus,

you will need lots of air-conditioning, since it reaches 900 degrees F
making Venus the hottest planet in the solar system.

  • Covered with thick clouds
  • Has toxic gases and yellow
    storm clouds
  • Air is mostly carbon dioxide
  • The heavy air will crush you
  • Water turns to steam
  • It's crators get smoothed
    over by lava from
    volcanoes
  • 1 year=225 earth days

On Earth,

you can drink water, since it's the only planet in the solar system that has water.

  • Temperature is just right
  • 3/4 of planet is covered with water
  • Distance from sun is just right
    and gives us seasons
  • Has storms from the hot and cold
  • Only place in the universe
    that has water
  • Only place on the earth
    that has signs of life
  • 1 year=365 days

On Mars,

you can go mountain climbing on the tallest mountain in the solar system called Olympus Mons.

  • Has 2 moons
  • 1 year=687 days
  • Has a volcano the size
    of New Mexico
  • Has a giant canyon
    called Valles Marinerus
  • Is a cold, dry dessert
  • The redness comes
    from iron that rusted
    when H20 was on
    the planet
  • Has dozens of volcanoes

On Jupiter,

you would have to go to bed every 5 hours, since Jupiter has the shortest day and spins so fast.

  • 1 year=4,331 years
  • Is only lasses, no solid land
  • Has colorful clouds of
    poisonous gasses
    and frozen water
  • Has 67 moons
  • It rotates very fast,
    every 10 hours
  • Has lightening strikes
    in it's clouds
  • Has a strong gravity hold
  • Has storms that last up
    to 100 years

On Saturn,

looking at the sky at night would be fun with 150 moons to see.

  • 1 year=10,756 days
  • Is only lasses, no solid land
  • Called the jewel of the
    Solar System
  • It's rings are made of bits
    of ice and rock
  • Has 150 moons
  • Has 7 rings
  • The purple ring has the
    largest rock and ice

On Uranus,

you will need a super warm coat, since Uranus is the coldest planet
in the Solar System at -340 degrees F.

  • 1 year=30,687 years
  • Used to be called
    George after the
    King of England
  • It spins on it's side
    like a necklace
  • Has 27 moons
  • Has 13 rings
  • William Herchel
    discovered this
    planet

On Neptune,

you will need a hoverboard to float around because Neptune has a gas surface, not solid.

  • 1 year=60,190 days
  • It's a dark, stormy planet
  • Has high winds that are
    4 times faster than
    on earth
  • A cloud named Scooter
    is like a constant
    hurricane
  • The seasons last 40 years
    on this planet
  • It has 14 moons

I choose Earth,

it's the best for me!

  • It has water to drink
  • It has four seasons
  • It's the only planet that
    supports life
